Word Game

Rules:

  • Each player takes turns to enter a word that starts with the letter of the last player's word's last letter.
  • Words are not to be repeated by any player during the game.
  • Each player has to enter a valid word within the time limit. Otherwise, they will lose a life.
  • A player is eliminated when they run out of lives.
  • The game ends when there only 1 player remaining.
